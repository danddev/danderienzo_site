A little bit about me

I’ve made my home base in New Jersey. My journey into tech started with a degree in Business Management. As I began sharing my experiences, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now, I’m making a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand, to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate and troubleshoot, and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer’s PSD and quickly and accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
